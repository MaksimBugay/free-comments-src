Our Mission

At Free Comments, we're driven by a vision to enhance how the world communicates online. While we've made substantial progress with limited resources, the journey ahead is ambitious and requires broader support to bring our full vision to life. This isn't just about building another tool—it's about creating a platform that empowers voices and connects ideas across various digital landscapes.

Why Your Support Matters

Every contribution, no matter the size, is crucial. You're not just donating, you're investing in a platform that offers immediate and free benefits to enhance your digital interactions. With additional funding, we can transform Free comments into a powerhouse for innovation and freedom of expression. Here's what we aim to introduce with your help:

  • Enhanced User Experience: Introduce non-commercial features that enrich usability. Remove comments per post limitation and increase comment live time.
  • Dynamic Interaction: Allow users to reply to comments, follow favorite commenters, and see comment impressions.
  • Cross-Device Accessibility: Develop a Free comments feed that can be shared across devices and browsers.
  • Engagement and Reward: Implement user karma, badges to enhance engagement.
  • Network Expansion: Extend support to other social networks.
  • Advanced Technology: Develop independent, blockchain-based solutions for decentralized and secure comment storage.
  • Design and Aesthetics: Roll out more advanced and visually appealing designs.
